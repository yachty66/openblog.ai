How to become a contributor?

  1. Create your contribution

    • use google docs for your contribution → it's a nice tool to make changes in documents
    • pick a topic about AI (philosophy, technical, ethical..)
    • start writing
    • follow our standards to hold everything in order → example

  2. Go on openblog.ai

  3. Join our Discord

  4. Share your link in the contributions channel

  5. Wait for feedback

  6. See your contribution on openblog.ai
